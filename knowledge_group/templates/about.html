Our Story

Building a Legacy of Academic Excellence

Knowledge Group Tuition was founded in 2010 with a simple but powerful vision — to provide every student in Ahmedabad access to quality education that goes beyond rote learning.

Over the past 14 years, we have helped more than 1500 students achieve their academic goals. Our approach is built on strong fundamentals, regular practice, and genuine care for each student's growth.

We are not just a tuition centre — we are a community where students feel safe to ask questions, make mistakes, and grow into confident learners.
